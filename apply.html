Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to join?

We look for practitioners with relevant certifications, professional experience, and alignment with our values of trust, empowerment, and community.

Is there a membership fee?

Yes, there is a monthly membership fee that helps us maintain the quality and curation of our directory. Pricing details will be shared during the application process.

How long does the review process take?

We typically review applications within 5-7 business days. Selected candidates will be contacted for a brief interview before final approval.

Can I update my profile after joining?

Absolutely! Once approved, you'll have access to a member dashboard where you can update your profile, bio, availability, and contact information.
